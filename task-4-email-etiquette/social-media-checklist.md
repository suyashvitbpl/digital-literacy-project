DO’s
● Do regular privacy audits. Check who can see your posts and tagged photos.
● Do think before you "Like" or Share. Even a "Like" is seen as an endorsement of that content.
● Do verify news from multiple reputable sources before reposting viral headlines
● Do be respectful of diverse backgrounds, languages, and cultures in your comments and posts.
● Do set screen-time limits. Use social media for connection, not just endless scrolling.
Don'ts
● Don't share sensitive info like your hostel room number, phone number, or exact live location
● Don't engage in or online arguments. Once it's in writing, it's permanent.
● Don't spread unverified rumors about exams, holidays, or campus events.
● Don't make jokes at the expense of others’ religious beliefs, caste, or hometowns
● Don't fall in the trap of reel life , real things are far truth than reel
